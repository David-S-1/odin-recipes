LA Galbi

Description

Galbi means “ribs” in Korean, and is a popular Korean dish served in many places. LA galbi is a style of galbi – barbecued beef short ribs – cut thinly (about ¼ inch thick) across the bones.

Ingredients

  • 3.5 lbs of LA style Beef Short Ribs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Water
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Toasted Sesame Oil
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Soybean Paste
  • Hot Pepper Paste
  • Green Onions
  • Perilla Leaves
  • Green Chili Pepper
  • Pear

Steps:

Prep the Meat
  1. Trim excess fat off of the short ribs
  2. Rinse the ribs in cold water for 10-20 minutes to remove blood

Make the Marinade
  1. In a bowl, add 1/3 cup Soy Sauce, 1/3 Cup Water/Cooking Wine, 1/4 Cup Honey or 1/3 cup Brown Sugar, and 1 Ts of Black Pepper
  2. Blend 1 Pear, 8 cloves of Garlic, 1 medium Onion, and 1 ts of Chopped Ginger
  3. Add it to your soy sauce base and add 2 tbs of Toasted Sesame Oil
  4. Rinse the ribs in freshc old water to remove any remaining bone fragments, and drain the water after
  5. Add the ribs to the marinade you made and mix
  6. Keep in the fridge overnight, or an hour if short on time

Dipping Sauce
  1. Mix 2 Tbs of Soy Bean Paste, 1 Tbs of Hot Pepper Paste, 1 stalk of chopped Green Onion, 1 clove of Minced Garlic, 1 Ts of Honey, 1 Ts of Sesame Oil, and 1 Ts of Sesame Seeds

Cook the Meat
  1. Grill, pan fry, or BBQ the ribs, takes around 5 minutes
  2. Cut the meat off the bone, or eat the meat from the bone, and enjoy!
