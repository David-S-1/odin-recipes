Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi Fried Rice, also known as Kimchi Bokkeumbap (김치 볶음밥) in Korean, is a popular type of Korean fried rice. It is predominantly made by stir frying kimchi (a traditional Korean side dish of fermented spicy napa cabbage) and rice, along with various seasonings.

But wait, the magic doesn’t end there! This versatile dish takes humble fried rice to new heights by incorporating additional ingredients such as diced meat and vegetables, enhancing its texture, flavor, and nutritional value.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Kimchi, cut into small pieces
  • 3 Cups of Steamed White Rice
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1/2 Tsp of Minced Garlic
  • 1/4 Cup of Kimchi Juice
  • 1/2 Tbsp of Sesame Oil
  • 1/2 Tbsp of Cooking Oil
  • 1-2 Tbsp of Roasted Sesame Seeds
  • 1/2 Stalk of Green Onion, thinly sliced
  • Roasted Shredded Seaweed

Steps:

Reminder: Cook the egg while cooking the rice so nothing gets cold!

  1. On medium-high heat preheat a pan or wok and add the cooking oil once heated
  2. Add garlic to the pan and stir for 10 seconds
  3. Add the Kimchi and stir until it is almost fully cooked
  4. Add the rice and smash it to make sure grains do not stick
  5. Add the kimchi juice to the rice evenly and mix thoroughly
  6. Add the sesame oil to the pain and mix well
  7. Remove pan from the heat
  8. Garnish with sesame seeds, green onion, and seaweed
  9. Add the cooked egg on top
  10. Enjoy!
